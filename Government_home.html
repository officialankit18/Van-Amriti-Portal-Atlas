Empowering Forest Communities with Technology

A unified digital platform integrating AI, GIS, and data analytics to streamline the Forest Rights Act (FRA) implementation and drive targeted development.

Discover Our Mission

The Challenge We Address

Scattered Data

Legacy records of forest rights are fragmented, non-digitized, and difficult to access or verify, creating significant administrative hurdles.

Lack of Visualization

There is no centralized, real-time visual repository (like an FRA Atlas) to map claims, granted titles, and available resources effectively.

Fragmented Systems

A critical disconnect exists between FRA data, satellite-based asset maps, and government development schemes, hindering targeted support.

Our Core Objectives

Digitize & Integrate Data

Standardize and digitize all legacy FRA data (claims, pattas) and integrate it with a dynamic FRA Atlas, including patta holders' shapefiles.

Create a Dynamic FRA Atlas

Develop a comprehensive FRA Atlas using AI and satellite data to visualize potential and granted FRA areas with high precision.

AI-Powered Asset Mapping

Use Remote Sensing and AI/ML to map capital and social assets (ponds, farms, forest resources) within FRA-holding villages.

Build a Decision Support System

Construct a DSS to recommend and layer Central Sector Schemes (CSS) for FRA beneficiaries, ensuring targeted and effective development.

AI & Technology Stack

Data Digitization

AI models to extract and standardize text from scanned documents, with NER for identifying key entities like names, locations, and claim status.

AI Asset Mapping

Computer Vision on satellite imagery to detect land use, water bodies, and homesteads using advanced ML models (CNNs, Random Forest).

WebGIS Integration

An interactive portal with filterable layers for rights, boundaries, assets, and real-time progress tracking on a geographical map.

Decision Support System

A rule-based and AI-enhanced engine to link FRA holders with eligible schemes and prioritize developmental interventions.

Project Deliverables

Core Outputs

  • AI-processed digital archive of all FRA claims and decisions.
  • An interactive FRA Atlas hosted on a WebGIS platform.
  • High-resolution, AI-generated asset maps for all FRA villages.
  • A functional Decision Support System (DSS) engine for policy and scheme layering.

Future Scope

  • Incorporate real-time satellite feeds for continuous monitoring.
  • Integrate IoT sensors for soil health and water quality data.
  • Enable a mobile-based feedback system for patta holders.
