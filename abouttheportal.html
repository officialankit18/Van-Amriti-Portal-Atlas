About the Portal

वनAmriti is a simple, secure and standards-based platform to digitize FRA records, map village assets using satellite data, visualize everything on a WebGIS Atlas and help departments layer schemes through an explainable Decision Support System.

Open Atlas FRA Guidelines

What is वनAmriti?

A unified portal to bring together legacy FRA claims, community/village boundaries, and asset layers (ponds, cropland, forests, homesteads) in one place. It supports districts with clean data, clear visuals, and transparent recommendations for scheme convergence.

Key features

Digitized FRA records

OCR + field extraction to standardize IFR/CR/CFR data with audit trails.

AI/RS asset mapping

Satellite-based layers of ponds, cropland, forest cover and built-up areas.

WebGIS Atlas

Filter by state/district/village, view FRA status and download summaries.

Explainable DSS

Simple rules highlight where to prioritize water assets or scheme linkages.

Digitization

  • Scan/OCR of legacy claims and orders
  • Standard fields: claimant, village, type, area, status
  • Quality checks and version history

AI/RS Mapping

  • Open satellite sources (Sentinel/Landsat)
  • Basic land-use classes and water bodies
  • Periodic updates for change tracking

Atlas

  • IFR/CR/CFR status by village
  • Asset overlays and simple charts
  • PDF/CSV exports for field teams

Decision Support

  • Clear rules and reasons (no black box)
  • Water-stress based prioritization
  • Ready for scheme convergence

How it fits together

High‑level flow: Digitization → Asset Mapping → Atlas → DSS.

Privacy & access

Public view shows only aggregated, PII‑free information. Named claimant data is visible only to authorized roles as per policy.

For districts

Clean lists for verification, easy maps for reviews, and simple recommendations to plan water/land works with communities.
