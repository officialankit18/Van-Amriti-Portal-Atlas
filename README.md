# -Amriti-Portal-Atlas
The वनAmriti Portal &amp; Atlas is an AI-powered FRA Atlas and Web GIS-based Decision Support platform that digitizes legacy FRA records, maps village assets from satellite imagery, and guides scheme delivery across Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Tripura, and Telangana.
**We are providing the following key features:**

Digitized FRA Records: Legacy FRA documents are digitized, standardized and cleaned for easy access.
AI-based Asset Mapping: Remote Sensing + AI to map agriculture, water bodies, forest cover, and homesteads from satellite imagery.
FRA Atlas: Interactive, real-time Atlas with IFR/CR/CFR layers, filters, search, and progress tracking for claims and titles.
Decision Support System:Recommends eligible Central Sector Schemes (CSS) (e.g., PM-KISAN, MGNREGA) with clear reasons - final approval stays with departments.
Role-Based Access and Security: NIC MeghRaj hosting, encryption, audit logs. Different users (villagers, NGOs, officials) get customized access.
